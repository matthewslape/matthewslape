Matthew Slape

My focus is to partner with people in bringing their creative ideas to life. In my designs, I value simplicity and purpose. I am not willing to design and put effort into something if there is not some sort of meaning behind it. I am driven to make a creative impact on the world that will last longer than myself. My work is pretty widespread, and stretches from brand identity to tattoo design. My inspiration comes from the creation of this Earth, and I am known for trying to replicate that in my designs. I put time and authenticity into my work, and feel that its main purpose is to open doors and connect people.
